About Me

Student & Engineer

I am an undergraduate junior studying Computer Science at Northwestern University. My interests lie in full-stack development and data science. I enjoy applying my passion for data and human-computer interaction in the lab and the classroom because each reflection means something. I like to design creative solutions in a fast-paced environment. As a proud first-generation Laotian-American student, I know how important it is for people to come together to support one another. Ever since I founded a data-driven chess league that brought hundreds of high school players together, I knew I could make an impact on the communities I am a part of by leveraging the technology I have available. Teamwork is one of my strengths due to my desire to strengthen the community. I prioritize my communication to facilitate feedback loops.

During my free time, you can catch me cheering on my favorite football team, Arsenal, improvising a pop tune to a jazz rendition on the piano, or watching a funny TV show. I also would like to explore many diverse world cultures and cuisines.
